# Intel Data Center Site Selection Dashboard (Tableau)

## Project Overview
This project explores regional energy production and sustainability metrics to support data center site selection decisions. The dashboard was built in Tableau and focuses on comparing regions based on overall energy output, renewable energy mix, and temporal generation trends.

The goal was to provide an interactive view of how energy availability and sustainability profiles vary across regions over time, helping inform infrastructure planning considerations.

## Objective
To evaluate potential regions for data center placement by analyzing:
- Net energy production by region
- Renewable energy as a percentage of total production
- Trends in energy generation by energy types across 2022

## Data & Tools
- Tool: Tableau
- Data: Regional energy production data for 2022, including breakdowns by energy type (e.g., fossil fuels, renewables)
- Approach: Interactive dashboard design to enable regional comparisons and time-based filtering

## Dashboard Structure
The dashboard consisted of three visual components:

1. Net Energy Production by Region (Bar Chart)
   Compared total net energy consumption across regions to identify areas with higher overall energy capacity.
  
2. Renewable Energy Share by Region (Bar Chart)
   Showed the proportion of energy generated from renewable sources, providing insight into sustainability and long-term energy viability.

3. Energy Generation Over Time by Region (Line Chart)
   Displayed monthly energy generation by energy form for 2022, with dropdown filters for region and month. This allowed for exploration of seasonal patterns and variability in energy production.

## Key Insights
- Regions with the highest net energy production also tended to exhibit more stable monthly generated profiles, indicating stronger capacity for continuous data center operations
- Renewable energy share varied substantially across regions, with some areas generating over 30% of total energy from renewable sources, improving long-term sustainability prospects
- Time-series analysis revealed seasonal fluctuations in certain energy types, highlighting potential variability risks that would need to be considered in infrastructure planning

## Notes
This project demonstrates how interactive dashboards can be used to evaluate operational and sustainability trade-offs in infrastructure planning, even when working with aggregated regional data.
